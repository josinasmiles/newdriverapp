Learning to Drive
Interacting with other passengers Particularly for teenage drivers, if you are a teen driver with other teens as passengers, statistics show you are more likely to have a crash.
Other Distractions • Searching for or moving an object in the vehicle. • Reading, writing or texting. • Personal grooming (combing hair, applying makeup). • Rubbernecking when passing a crash scene or a work zone. • Looking at people, objects or events happening off of the roadway.
