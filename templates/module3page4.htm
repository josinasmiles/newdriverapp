DRIVING DISTRACTIONS
Anything that causes you to 1) take your attention away from driving, 2) take your eyes off of the road or 3) take your hands off of the wheel is a distraction.
Eating, drinking and smoking These all create safety problems because they often require you to take your hands off of the wheel and take your eyes off of the road. Drivers who eat or drink while driving have trouble controlling their vehicles and staying in their lane.
Adjusting audio devices radio, cassette, CD, Ipod/Mp3 player. NOTE: headphones/ear pieces can only be used in one ear for communication purposes.
Talking on a cell phone whether it is hands-free or not
