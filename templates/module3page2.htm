Learning to Drive
Fasten your seat belt Fasten both your lap and shoulder belts on every trip.
Secure loose items in the passenger compartment In a crash, loose items in your vehicle become projectiles that continue to travel the same speed your vehicle was moving before the impact. Put heavier objects, such as backpacks, luggage and tools in your trunk, whenever possible, or secure them with the vehicleâ€™s seat belts. Never store items on the rear window deck.
Adjust the rearview and side mirrors To remove blind spots around your vehicle, the rearview and side mirrors may have to be adjusted differently for each driver. The rearview mirror should be set so you can see the traffic directly behind your vehicle. The side mirror(s) should be set so when you lean left/right you can just barely see the side of your vehicle. Set the mirrors after you adjust your seat.
