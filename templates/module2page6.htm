REGULATORY SIGNS
Regulatory signs tell you what you must do, or must not do, according to the law.
The STOP sign is the only octagon-shaped (8-sided) sign you see on the highway. At an intersection with a STOP sign, you must stop completely, check for pedestrians and cross traffic to clear the intersection before you go again.
This sign is also used in conjunction with stop signs. This sign allows motorists to make the right turn without stopping.
A FOUR-WAY STOP sign means there are four stop signs at this intersection. Traffic from all four directions must stop. The first vehicle to reach the intersection should move forward first.
